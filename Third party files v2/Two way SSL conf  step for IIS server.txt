Cert installation procedure for IIS
=========================================


## Step 01 - Import the ROOT and Intermediate authorities to ca - ORDER matters (Root,Policy,Issuer)


## Step 02
cd /usr/jdk/jdk1.7.0_21/jre/bin
keytool -genkey -alias <name>  -keyalg RSA -keysize 2048 –keystore <name>.jks  (generates key and jks)
keytool -certreq -alias <name>   -keyalg RSA -file <name>  .csr -keystore <name> .jks(generate the csr from the key in jks, above command)

## Step 03 - add Root and intermediate authorities into keystore

keytool -importcert -file LABROOTCA01.cer -keystore <name>  .jks -alias "<name>  "
keytool -importcert -file LABPOLICYCA01.cer -keystore <name>  .jks -alias "<name>  "
keytool -importcert -file LABISS -keystore <name>  .jks -alias "<name>  "

## Step 04 - add our signed certificate into jks
keytool -importcert -file <name>  .cer -keystore <name>  .jks -alias "<name> "
